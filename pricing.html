FAQ

A few helpful notes before inquiring.

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes. Lexie Artistry is based in the San Francisco Bay Area and available for California and destination weddings.

Is the date reserved after inquiry?

No. Availability is confirmed after the schedule, service details, contract, and deposit are completed.

Do packages include touch-ups?

Touch-ups are included in the 5-hour, 8-hour, and 10-hour Bride + Groom packages. Shorter services do not include touch-ups.
