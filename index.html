Natural, refined, camera-ready beauty for Bay Area brides.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes. Lexie Artistry provides on-location bridal hair and makeup services in the Bay Area and Northern California. Please share the getting-ready address for a travel fee quote.

Do you offer bridal trials?

Yes. Bridal trial makeup is available for $400.

Is there an early morning fee?

There is no early fee after 7:00 AM. Before 7:00 AM, the early fee is $60 per 30 minutes.

Can you accommodate bridesmaids and family members?

Yes. Bridesmaid and mother services are available for $200 per person. Please include the total number of people when submitting an inquiry.

How do I check availability?

Use the inquiry form below with your wedding date, location, number of services, and ready-by time. Lexie will confirm availability and pricing after reviewing the details.
